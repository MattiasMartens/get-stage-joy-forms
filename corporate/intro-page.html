What We Do

At Inspired Coaching, we confront ineffective communication with a comprehensive training system that begins right at the source: the connection between the mind that forms the message, and the body that voices it with breath.

The limits of your voice-body instrument are the limits of your power as a speaker.

Our programs give clients an environment where they can break through their limits, with all the support they need to make those breakthroughs permanent.

What We Offer

We offer training in three main skill categories: Presentations, Sales, and Teams and Leadership.

We are committed to providing you with a training package that is tailored to the needs of your business. Once we’ve gotten to know each other at a Lunch and Learn seminar, we may recommend one or more of the following training types:

  • Intensive

    A laser-focused session addressing a single skill or pain point. Could be half-day, full-day, or multiple days in duration.

  • Comprehensive

    A recurring program to develop a whole suite of communication skills for your team. Sessions may be scheduled on a weekly, monthly, or quarterly basis and include a combination of intensives, consultations, and team exercises.
